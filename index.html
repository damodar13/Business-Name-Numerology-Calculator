What is Numerology?

Numerology is an ancient metaphysical science that studies the relationship between numbers and events in our lives. It is based on the belief that numbers carry unique vibrations and energies that influence our personalities, decisions, and even the success of our businesses.

In the context of business, numerology helps you understand the energetic blueprint of your business name. By calculating the destiny number of your business name, you can uncover its strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for growth.

How Does Numerology Work for Business Names?

Numerology assigns a numerical value to each letter in your business name. These values are then added together and reduced to a single-digit number (or a master number like 11, 22, or 33), known as the destiny number. This number holds the key to understanding the energy and potential of your business.

Steps to Calculate Your Business Name Numerology:

  1. Assign Numbers to Letters: Use the Chaldean numerology chart to assign a number to each letter in your business name.
  2. Sum the Numbers: Add all the numbers together to get a total sum.
  3. Reduce to a Single Digit: If the total is a double-digit number, reduce it to a single digit by adding the digits together (e.g., 36 → 3 + 6 = 9).
  4. Interpret the Destiny Number: Each destiny number has a unique meaning that reveals the strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for your business.
